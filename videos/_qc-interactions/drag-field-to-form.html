dragFieldToForm(fieldSlug)

Cursor glides to a field button in the left palette, presses, carries a ghost element onto the form canvas, releases. Mid-drag the canvas FLIP-grows to make room. DOM-only. The starting form layout is replaceable — defaults to Contact Us (3 fields). Pick a different form profile to start from a different layout. Source: wpforms-interactions.js · ← back to index
