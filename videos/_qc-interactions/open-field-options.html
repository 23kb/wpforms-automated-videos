openFieldOptions(fieldId)

Cursor glides to a field on the canvas, clicks, and the left sidebar tab swaps from "Add Fields" to "Field Options" with that field's option panel visible. DOM-only — no snapshot swap. Available field IDs in builder-fields: 48 (Name), 49 (Email), 50 (Textarea), and many more. Source: wpforms-interactions.js · ← back to index
