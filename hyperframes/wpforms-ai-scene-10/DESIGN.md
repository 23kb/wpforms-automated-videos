# WPForms AI Scene 10 Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Premium SaaS launch finale for WPForms AI: a luminous white and soft-blue space where generated field fragments crystallize into a finished online form, then become a polished browser preview with a calm centered brand signature.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F7FBFF`
- Ink: `#101828`
- Support text: `#475467`
- WPForms blue: `#056AAB`
- AI blue: `#0399ED`
- Accent orange: `#E27730`
- Glass white: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

- Primary: Inter, system UI fallback
- Treatment: crisp SaaS UI labels, centered premium lockup, compact readable browser chrome

## Motion Rules

- The main event is transformation: fragments to form stack to finished form to browser preview.
- Use finite GSAP motion only, with staggered reveals, blur-to-sharp transitions, and soft glow sweeps.
- Keep the end frame alive through settling motion, not loops.

## What NOT To Do

- No left-right split-screen end card.
- No big orange CTA button.
- No dark cyber palette.
- No fake WPForms admin UI.
- No infinite repeat animations.
